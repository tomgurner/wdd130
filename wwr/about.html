History

The river was already here when we showed up so we decided "why not float it?" We did, and we've been doing so since 1998!

We were founded in 1998 by 4 best friends from Texas - Hank, Dale, Bill, and Boomhauer. When you choose to float the Santiam River with us, you know there will always be good times and plenty of Alamo to be had.
